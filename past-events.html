1st Data-Driven Modelling and Simulation workshop

On the 23rd of August 2023, a one-day workshop was organized and hosted at the CSIR International Convention Centre Brummeria Pretoria. The workshop's goal was to develop and offer a platform where researchers, academics, and business professionals could form cooperative research networks to share their research interests, experiences, and resources as well as their expertise in mentoring and supervising research and development projects related to the digital fourth industrial revolution in South Africa..
