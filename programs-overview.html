Current Projects

The M&S Hub aims to facilitate and strengthen South African Modelling and Simulation R&D activities across the work domains to demonstrate their capability as tools for accelerated innovation and industrialisation

Models and Simulation Methods Development

Modelling and simulation solves real-world problems without any disruption or disturbances to the real-world system. It is more a efficient and cost-effective means to determine the impact of a variable change in the system.

View Project

Multi Phenomena Multiscale Modelling and Simulation Technologies

Multi phenomena multiscale modelling and simulation technologies encompass a variety of approaches aimed at capturing complex systems' behaviour across multiple scales.

View Project

Model Verification and Validation

Model verification and validation are the key processes for quantifying and establishing credibility in numerical models and simulations. The academic institutions and industries require better modelling tools for model verification and validation of product performance, including validated models and verified simulation methods.

View Project

Data-Driven Modelling and Simulation Technologies

The availability of huge volumes of data along with advancements in computational resources and data analytics are expected to have a revolutionary impact on modelling, simulation and decision making.
