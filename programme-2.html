Multi Phenomena Multiscale Modelling and Simulation Technologies

Multi phenomena multiscale modelling and simulation technologies encompass a variety of approaches aimed at capturing complex systems' behaviour across multiple scales. This includes discrete-event systems models, which focus on events occurring at distinct points in time, Agent-based Simulation Models, which simulate the actions and interactions of autonomous agents, and continuous modelling and simulation, which deal with systems evolving over continuous time and space. Integration of these diverse modelling and simulation techniques is crucial for comprehensively understanding and predicting system behaviour. Various integration models exist, such as hybrid approaches combining discrete-event and continuous models, or frameworks that incorporate agent-based models within larger-scale simulations. These integration models enable us to tackle multifaceted problems and gain insights into systems characterized by diverse phenomena and scales.
