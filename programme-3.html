Program 3

Model Verification and Validation

Model verification and validation are the key processes for quantifying and establishing credibility in numerical models and simulations. The academic institutions and industries require better modelling tools for model verification and validation of product performance, including validated models and verified simulation methods. Also, availability of more structured and validated information is essential for the ability to reliably simulate options and make sound data-based business decisions. Model validation ensures that the physics implemented in the model is assessed by checking accuracy of the prediction against experimental measurements whereas model verification ensures that the model is correctly implemented by checking model inputs and outputs with simplified example cases. This project will focus on the development of standards and plans for model verification and validation, to promote the development of high-fidelity models in various fields.
